Purpose of the Field Study

[span_3](start_span)

This field study was conducted as part of our Selling & Negotiation course at S.P. Mandali's WeSchool[span_3](end_span). [span_4](start_span)The objective was to step beyond classroom theory and observe how real buying, selling, and negotiation actually unfold across different retail environments in Mumbai[span_4](end_span). [span_5](start_span)By visiting four distinct markets—a street flea market, a leather artisan cluster, a wholesale commodity hub, and an organised mall—our group aimed to document first-hand how sellers pitch and how buyers respond[span_5](end_span).
